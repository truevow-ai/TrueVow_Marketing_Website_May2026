📋 How to Use

  1. Publish on Platform: First, publish your content on LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, or Instagram.
  2. Select Platform: Choose the platform from the dropdown (left side).
  3. Select Type: Choose the content type from the dropdown (right side) - options change based on platform.
  4. Copy URL: Copy the full URL from your published post.
  5. Fill Form: Enter the title, teaser, and paste the URL above.
  6. Set Status: Choose "Published" to make it visible on the blog hub immediately.
  7. Submit: Click "Add Content" - it will appear on the blog page automatically!
