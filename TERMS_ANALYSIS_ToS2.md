### **1. FORMAL RECITALS (NEW SECTION - BEFORE SECTION 1)**

**CURRENT STATE:**

**PROPOSED ADDITION FROM ToS #2:**

✅ **RECITALS - Traditional Legal Format**
- **Why:** Establishes context, parties, corporate structure, and intent in formal legal language (standard for complex agreements)
- **Language:** WHEREAS clauses that:
  - Identify TrueVow Global Technologies FZ-LLC (UAE) as contracting party
  - Explain Firm is a licensed attorney/law firm
  - Clarify TrueVow Global Holdings LLC (Nevis) owns IP

```
WHEREAS, TrueVow Global Technologies FZ-LLC, a limited liability company duly organized and existing under the laws of the United Arab Emirates, with its principal place of business at Dubai Silicon Oasis, Dubai, United Arab Emirates ("TrueVow"), operates a technology platform designed to assist licensed legal professionals...
```

- **Why:** Explicit, detailed risk allocation for Ancillary ML Tools
- **Language:** Firm expressly acknowledges and agrees that:
  - **(a)** outputs from Ancillary ML Tools may contain inaccuracies in transcription, timing, voice inflection, code syntax, authentication tokens, or data formatting

  - **(c)** TrueVow is **not the developer, licensor, or controller** of the underlying models and systems and **makes no representation or warranty** regarding accuracy, reliability, non-infringement, merchantability, or fitness for a particular purpose
  - **(d)** Firm's **exclusive remedy** for defects, errors, or failures in Ancillary ML Tools lies **solely against the third-party vendor**, not TrueVow
  - **(e)** Firm's data is **not used to train** the vendor's models, per their published policies
- **Impact:** STRONGEST possible liability shield for third-party ML tools
