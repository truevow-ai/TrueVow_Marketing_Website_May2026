**Current:**
- Headline: "Stop Losing $5,150-$20,600/Month in Missed Cases"
- Recovery: "Average firm may recover up to $20,600/month in additional revenue"

## FINAL RECOMMENDATION

### ✅ **APPROVED WITH MODIFICATION**

**Recommended Version:**
- **Headline:** "Stop Losing ~$5,150 per Missed Case"
- **Recovery:** "Average firm may recover up to ~$20,600/month in additional revenue (3-4 cases/month)"

**Why This Works:**
1. ✅ Eliminates visual repetition (different units: "per case" vs "per month")
2. ✅ "Per case" is more tangible and emotionally compelling
3. ✅ "~" adds credibility (shows it's an estimate, not exact)
4. ✅ Monthly recovery shows compound effect
5. ✅ Adding "(3-4 cases/month)" bridges the gap between unit and monthly

**Alternative (If you want to keep monthly in headline):**
- **Headline:** "Stop Losing Up to $20,600/Month in Missed Cases"
- **Recovery:** "Average firm may recover up to ~$20,600/month in additional revenue"

This also eliminates repetition by using "up to" instead of range format.
