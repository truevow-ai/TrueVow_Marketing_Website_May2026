# Standard Cost Figures for All Landing Pages

**Last Updated:** 2024-12-19  
**Source:** Industry research and verified data

## Standardized Cost Anchors

### 1. Human Receptionist (Total Cost of Employment)
- **Annual Cost:** $48,000/year
- **Monthly Cost:** $4,000/month
- **Breakdown:**
  - Base salary: $42,950/year (industry average per Salary.com)
  - Benefits & overhead: ~12% additional cost
  - Total: $48,000/year ($4,000/month)

**Rationale:** This figure represents the total cost of employment including base salary, benefits, payroll taxes, and basic overhead. It's conservative and defensible based on industry data showing $38,000-$52,000 base salary range, with total cost typically 1.15-1.25x base salary.

---

### 2. Answering Service (Mid-Tier Live Receptionist)
- **Monthly Cost:** $1,500/month
- **Annual Cost:** $18,000/year
- **Service Type:** Live receptionist service (not basic message-taking)

**Rationale:** Industry data shows:
- Basic message-taking: $100-$400/month
- Live receptionist services: $300-$1,500/month
- Premium services (Smith.ai, etc.): $1,500-$3,000/month

We use $1,500/month as it represents a mid-tier live receptionist service that most firms would consider as an alternative to TrueVow.

---

### 3. Marketing Ad Spend Loss (Abandoned Calls)
- **Monthly Ad Spend:** $5,000/month
- **Voicemail Abandonment Rate:** 38%
- **Monthly Loss:** $1,900/month
- **Annual Loss:** $22,800/year

**Calculation:** $5,000 × 38% = $1,900/month lost

**Rationale:** 
- $5,000/month is a reasonable average for solo/small law firm marketing spend
- 38% abandonment rate is industry standard for voicemail
- This represents the portion of marketing spend that's wasted when calls go to voicemail and never call back

---

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Always use these exact figures** when comparing TrueVow to alternatives
2. **Present as total cost of employment** for receptionist (not just salary)
3. **Specify "mid-tier live receptionist service"** when referencing answering service cost
4. **Include the calculation** ($5,000 × 38% = $1,900) when showing marketing ad spend loss
