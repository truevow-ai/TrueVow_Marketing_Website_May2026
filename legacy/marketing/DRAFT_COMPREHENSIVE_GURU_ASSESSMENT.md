## 4. PRICING STRATEGY - COMPREHENSIVE ANALYSIS

### 4.1 Expert Analysis (Applying Rafi Muhammad, Alex Hormozi, Jason Cohen Pricing Principles)

**Key Considerations:**
1. **Free offerings can devalue product** - need to signal value
2. **Attorneys are price-sensitive** - but value quality
3. **Habit formation critical** - need regular use
4. **Network effects limited** - unlike SETTLE (data improves with users)

**Option Analysis:**

#### Option A: First Year Free, Then Paid
**Score: 6/10**
- ✅ Maximum adoption
- ❌ No revenue Year 1
- ❌ May devalue product
- ❌ Hard conversion after free

**Verdict:** Risky. Only if you need maximum adoption for network effects (DRAFT doesn't have network effects like SETTLE).

#### Option B: First 12 Documents Free Per Month, Then $29/Doc
**Score: 9.5/10**
- ✅ Signals value (not completely free)
- ✅ Covers most needs (12/month = ~3 cases/month)
- ✅ Creates habit (regular use)
- ✅ Revenue from heavy users
- ✅ Clear upgrade path
- ✅ Maintains value perception

**Verdict:** BEST OPTION. Balances adoption and value.

#### Option C: Freemium Tiers (Free/Pro/Enterprise)
**Score: 7/10**
- ✅ Clear value ladder
- ✅ Revenue from Day 1
- ❌ Complex (3 tiers)
- ❌ May confuse users
- ❌ Requires feature differentiation

**Verdict:** Good for Year 2+, too complex for launch.

#### Option D: Pay-Per-Use Only ($29/doc)
**Score: 5/10**
- ✅ Simple
- ✅ Revenue from Day 1
- ❌ High friction (pay before trying)
- ❌ May limit adoption
- ❌ No habit formation

**Verdict:** Too much friction. Won't build adoption.

#### Option E: Credits System
**Score: 7/10**
- ✅ Volume discounts
- ✅ Prepaid revenue
- ❌ Upfront payment barrier
- ❌ Complex

**Verdict:** Good for heavy users, but adds complexity.

### 4.2 Recommended Pricing Model

**Phase 1: Launch (Q1 2027)**
- **First 12 documents FREE per month**
- Unlimited for Founding Members
- $29/document after 12 (if needed)

**Phase 2: Year 2 (2028)**
- **First 12 documents FREE per month** (maintain free tier)
- $29/document after 12
- Unlimited for Founding Members (always)

**Phase 3: Year 3+ (2029+)**
- Consider premium tiers if needed
- Advanced features (team collaboration, etc.)

**Why This Works:**
1. **12/month covers most solo/small firm needs** (~3 cases/month)
2. **Signals value** (not unlimited, but generous)
3. **Creates habit** (regular monthly use)
4. **Revenue from heavy users** (who exceed 12/month)
5. **Maintains value perception** (not "free tool")
6. **Rewards Founding Members** (unlimited = exclusivity)

**Pricing Score: 9.5/10**
