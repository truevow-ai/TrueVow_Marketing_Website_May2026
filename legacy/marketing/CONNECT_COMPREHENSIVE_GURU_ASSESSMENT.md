## 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

### 1.1 What CONNECT Is

**CONNECT** is a professional referral network platform that facilitates:
1. **Attorney-to-Attorney Referrals:** Connect with other attorneys for case referrals
2. **Attorney-to-Medical Provider Referrals:** Connect with medical providers (with strict compliance safeguards)
3. **Case Management:** Track referrals, manage relationships, ensure compliance

**Key Features:**
- Directory of licensed professionals (attorneys, medical providers)
- Introduction-only platform (does NOT facilitate fee arrangements)
- Compliance safeguards (ABA Model Rules, HIPAA, state bar rules)
- Zero-knowledge architecture (minimal data sharing)
- Bar-compliant design (pre-clearance required)

### 1.2 The Problem CONNECT Solves

**Problem 1: Finding Qualified Referral Attorneys**
- Solo attorneys get cases outside their practice area
- No easy way to find qualified attorneys in other jurisdictions
- Referral networks are expensive ($500-$2,000/month) or non-existent
- Word-of-mouth referrals are unreliable and slow

**Problem 2: Managing Referral Relationships**
- No system to track referrals
- No way to ensure compliance (fee division, client consent)
- No visibility into referral outcomes
- Manual processes = compliance risk

**Problem 3: Medical Provider Connections**
- PI attorneys need medical providers for treatment
- No compliant way to connect with providers
- Risk of violating fee-sharing prohibitions (Rule 5.4)
- HIPAA compliance concerns

**Market Size:**
- **TAM:** ~200,000 solo/small law firms in US
- **SAM:** ~50,000 active referrers (refer 1+ cases/year)
- **SOM (Year 1):** ~2,000-5,000 attorneys (4-10% of SAM)

## 7. COMPLIANCE & RISK MITIGATION

### 7.1 Bar Compliance (Critical)

**ABA Model Rules:**
- **Rule 1.5(e):** CONNECT does NOT facilitate fee division (attorneys enter separate agreements)
- **Rule 5.4:** CONNECT does NOT share legal fees (flat platform fees only)
- **Rule 7.2(b):** CONNECT does NOT pay for referrals
- **Rule 1.6:** CONNECT shares only non-privileged information
- **Rule 1.7:** Attorneys conduct own conflicts checks

**State Bar Pre-Clearance:**
- **Required:** Pre-clearance from state bars (e.g., FL Bar Ethics Opinion 24-1)
- **Status:** Pre-launch (do not use until officially launched)
- **Safeguards:** Compliance framework built-in

### 7.2 HIPAA Compliance

**Medical Provider Connections:**
- **BAA Required:** If PHI shared, Business Associate Agreement required
- **Templates Provided:** CONNECT provides BAA templates
- **Encryption:** PHI encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Attorney Responsibility:** Attorneys responsible for executing BAAs and obtaining client consent
