# DRAFT + INTAKE Integration Strategy
## Ensuring Mass Adoption: Bundling & Cross-Sell Strategy

---

## Executive Summary

**Problem:** DRAFT may not get mass adoption like INTAKE because:
- DRAFT is preventive (future risk) vs INTAKE is urgent (immediate loss)
- Attorneys may skip validation to save $29
- DRAFT works for ALL practice areas, not just PI

**Solution:** Bundle DRAFT with INTAKE to drive adoption through existing user base.

---

## 1. THE OPPORTUNITY

### Current INTAKE User Base:
- **Target:** Solo & small law firms (≤21 attorneys)
- **Practice Areas:** Personal Injury, Family Law, Immigration, Criminal Defense, Employment, Real Estate, Bankruptcy, Estate Planning, Workers' Comp
- **Behavior:** Already using TrueVow platform, trust established, workflow integrated

### DRAFT's Universal Appeal:
- **Works for ALL practice areas** (not just PI)
- **Every attorney files documents** (complaints, motions, responses, etc.)
- **Compliance is universal** (all courts have filing requirements)

**Key Insight:** If attorneys trust INTAKE, they'll trust DRAFT. Bundle them together.

---

## 2. BUNDLING STRATEGIES

### Strategy A: INTAKE Users Get DRAFT FREE (RECOMMENDED)

**Model:**
- **INTAKE users:** Get 3 FREE DRAFT validations/month (always)
- **After 3 free:** $19/document (discounted rate for INTAKE users)
- **Non-INTAKE users:** Standard DRAFT pricing ($29/document after 12 free signup)

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Zero friction (already using TrueVow)
- ✅ Creates habit (3 free/month encourages regular use)
- ✅ Cross-sell opportunity (DRAFT users may try INTAKE)
- ✅ Value add (makes INTAKE more valuable)

**Revenue Impact:**
- INTAKE users get free tier (no revenue loss - they weren't paying anyway)
- Heavy users pay $19/document (revenue from power users)
- Non-INTAKE users pay full price ($29/document)

**Adoption Rate:** Expected 80-90% of INTAKE users will use DRAFT

---

### Strategy B: DRAFT Included in INTAKE Subscription

**Model:**
- **INTAKE users:** DRAFT included FREE (unlimited validations)
- **Non-INTAKE users:** Standard DRAFT pricing

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Maximum adoption (100% of INTAKE users get DRAFT)
- ✅ Competitive advantage (competitors don't offer this)
- ✅ Increases INTAKE value (makes switching harder)

**Why It Might Not:**
- ❌ No additional revenue from INTAKE users
- ❌ May cannibalize standalone DRAFT sales
- ❌ Higher support costs (more users)

**Adoption Rate:** Expected 95-100% of INTAKE users will use DRAFT

---

### Strategy C: Discounted Bundle (INTAKE + DRAFT)

**Model:**
- **INTAKE only:** $29/booking
- **DRAFT only:** $29/document
- **INTAKE + DRAFT bundle:** $29/booking + $19/document (DRAFT discounted)

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Incentivizes bundle purchase
- ✅ Maintains revenue from both products
- ✅ Creates switching cost (harder to leave)

**Why It Might Not:**
- ❌ Still requires separate payment decision
- ❌ May not drive adoption (attorneys may skip DRAFT to save $19)

**Adoption Rate:** Expected 40-60% of INTAKE users will use DRAFT

---

### Strategy D: Automatic Validation (Workflow Integration)

**Model:**
- **INTAKE users:** When case is booked, DRAFT automatically validates related documents
- **Seamless workflow:** INTAKE → DRAFT → File
- **Pricing:** Included in INTAKE subscription or $19/document

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Zero friction (automatic)
- ✅ Creates habit (part of workflow)
- ✅ Maximum adoption (can't skip it)

**Why It Might Not:**
- ❌ May feel invasive (automatic validation)
- ❌ Technical complexity (integration required)
- ❌ May validate documents attorneys don't want validated

**Adoption Rate:** Expected 90-100% (if automatic)

## 8. RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Cannibalization
**Problem:** Free DRAFT for INTAKE users may reduce standalone DRAFT sales

**Mitigation:**
- Non-INTAKE users still pay full price
- Heavy users pay discounted rate ($19 vs $29)
- Increases INTAKE retention (more valuable)

### Risk 2: Support Costs
**Problem:** More DRAFT users = more support tickets

**Mitigation:**
- Zero-knowledge architecture = fewer support issues
- Automated validation = self-service
- INTAKE users already familiar with TrueVow = less training

### Risk 3: Technical Complexity
**Problem:** Integrating DRAFT with INTAKE may be complex

**Mitigation:**
- Start with simple integration (button in dashboard)
- Add workflow integration later (Phase 2)
- Test with beta users first

**Expected Result:** 80-90% of INTAKE users will use DRAFT, driving mass adoption across all practice areas.
