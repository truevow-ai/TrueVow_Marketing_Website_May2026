# DRAFT Pricing Strategy - Strategic Options Analysis
## TrueVow DRAFT™ Pricing Model Recommendations

---

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Maximize adoption while maintaining product value and ensuring sustainable revenue. Balance between "freemium" model (builds habit) vs. "paid from start" (signals value).

**Key Consideration:** Free offerings can lose perceived value. Need to find the sweet spot between adoption and value signaling.

---

## Option 1: First Year Free, Then Paid

### Model
- **Year 1:** Unlimited document validations FREE
- **Year 2+:** $29/document or subscription model

### Pros
- ✅ Maximum adoption in Year 1 (no friction)
- ✅ Builds strong habit (attorneys get used to using it)
- ✅ Creates switching cost (they're dependent on it)
- ✅ High retention after Year 1 (they've integrated it into workflow)
- ✅ Can upsell other TrueVow services during free period

### Cons
- ❌ No revenue in Year 1
- ❌ May attract non-serious users (free riders)
- ❌ Could devalue the product (if it's free, is it valuable?)
- ❌ Hard to convert to paid after free (entitlement mindset)
- ❌ May not signal quality/value

### Best For
- **Early stage:** When you need maximum adoption and testimonials
- **Network effects:** When more users = better product (like SETTLE)
- **Competitive moat:** When you want to lock in users before competitors

### Recommendation Score: 7/10
**Verdict:** Good for Year 1, but risky for long-term value perception.

---

## Option 2: First 12 Documents Free Per Month, Then $29/Document

### Model
- **Monthly Allowance:** 12 document validations FREE per month
- **After 12:** $29 per additional document
- **Rollover:** Unused validations don't roll over (or do, up to 24 max)

### Pros
- ✅ Signals value (not completely free)
- ✅ Covers most solo/small firm needs (12 docs/month = ~3 cases/month)
- ✅ Creates urgency (use them or lose them)
- ✅ Revenue from heavy users (who need more than 12)
- ✅ Habit-forming (they use it regularly)
- ✅ Clear upgrade path (need more? Pay per use)

### Cons
- ❌ May limit adoption (some attorneys might not try it)
- ❌ Could frustrate users who hit the limit
- ❌ Requires tracking/documentation system
- ❌ May encourage gaming (splitting documents to stay under limit)

### Best For
- **Mature product:** When you want balanced adoption + revenue
- **Solo/small firms:** When 12/month covers most needs
- **Pay-per-use model:** When you want to avoid subscriptions

### Recommendation Score: 9/10
**Verdict:** Best balance of adoption and value signaling. Recommended.

---

## Option 3: Freemium with Premium Tiers

### Model
- **Free Tier:** 5 document validations/month (basic validation)
- **Pro Tier ($49/month):** Unlimited validations + advanced features
- **Enterprise Tier ($199/month):** Unlimited + team features + priority support

### Pros
- ✅ Clear value ladder
- ✅ Revenue from Day 1 (from Pro users)
- ✅ Free tier drives adoption
- ✅ Premium features justify upgrade
- ✅ Predictable revenue (subscription)

### Cons
- ❌ More complex (3 tiers to manage)
- ❌ May confuse users (which tier do I need?)
- ❌ Free tier might be too limited (5 docs/month)
- ❌ Requires feature differentiation

### Best For
- **Mature product:** When you have advanced features to differentiate
- **SaaS model:** When you want predictable MRR
- **Enterprise focus:** When you want to target larger firms

### Recommendation Score: 6/10
**Verdict:** Good long-term, but complex for launch. Better for Year 2+.

---

## Option 4: Pay-Per-Use with Credits System

### Model
- **Credits:** Buy credits in bulk (10 credits = $250 = $25/doc)
- **Volume Discounts:** 50 credits = $1,000 ($20/doc), 100 credits = $1,800 ($18/doc)
- **Free Trial:** 3 credits free on signup

### Pros
- ✅ Volume discounts incentivize bulk purchase
- ✅ Predictable revenue (prepaid)
- ✅ Free trial reduces friction
- ✅ Clear value (credits = value stored)
- ✅ No monthly commitment

### Cons
- ❌ Upfront payment barrier
- ❌ Credits can expire (frustration)
- ❌ More complex than simple pay-per-use
- ❌ May not work for occasional users

### Best For
- **Heavy users:** When attorneys file many documents
- **Bulk purchasing:** When firms want to prepay
- **Cash flow:** When you want upfront revenue

### Recommendation Score: 7/10
**Verdict:** Good for heavy users, but adds complexity.

## Option 5: Hybrid Model (RECOMMENDED)

### Model
- **Year 1:** First 12 documents FREE per month (unlimited for Founding Members)
- **Year 2+:** First 12 documents FREE per month, then $29/document
- **Founding Members:** Always get unlimited FREE (as part of Founding Member package)

### Why This Works
1. **Year 1 Free 12/month:**
   - Builds adoption without devaluing product
   - Covers most solo/small firm needs
   - Creates habit and dependency
   - Signals value (not completely free)

2. **Year 2+ Paid After 12:**
   - Revenue from heavy users
   - Maintains free tier for light users
   - Clear upgrade path
   - Sustainable business model

3. **Founding Member Benefit:**
   - Rewards early adopters
   - Creates exclusivity
   - Drives SETTLE contributions (3 settlements/month)

### Implementation
- **Tracking:** Simple counter (documents validated this month)
- **Reset:** Monthly reset (1st of each month)
- **Notification:** Alert at 10/12 documents used
- **Upgrade:** Clear CTA when limit reached

### Pros
- ✅ Best of both worlds (adoption + revenue)
- ✅ Maintains value perception
- ✅ Habit-forming (regular use)
- ✅ Clear upgrade path
- ✅ Rewards Founding Members
- ✅ Sustainable long-term

### Cons
- ❌ Requires tracking system
- ❌ May frustrate users who hit limit
- ❌ Need clear communication about limits

### Recommendation Score: 10/10
**Verdict:** Best option. Balances adoption, value, and revenue.

## Value Preservation Strategies

### How to Prevent Free from Devaluing Product

1. **Limit the Free Tier:**
   - 12/month is generous but not unlimited
   - Signals "this is valuable, but we're giving you a taste"

2. **Show Value:**
   - Display "You saved $X in time" after each validation
   - Show "This validation prevented Y errors"
   - Highlight malpractice risk reduction

3. **Create Urgency:**
   - "12 free validations this month" (not unlimited)
   - "3 remaining this month" notifications
   - "Upgrade for unlimited" when limit reached

4. **Premium Positioning:**
   - "Professional validation tool" (not "free tool")
   - Emphasize quality and accuracy
   - Highlight zero-knowledge architecture (premium feature)

5. **Founding Member Exclusivity:**
   - Unlimited FREE only for Founding Members
   - Creates FOMO and exclusivity
   - Rewards early adopters

## Competitive Analysis

### How Competitors Price

**Clio/PracticePanther:** $39-$99/month (subscription, includes document storage)
**Westlaw/Lexis:** $2K-$8K/year (subscription, research + some validation)
**HotDocs:** $1K-$5K/year (subscription, document generation)

**DRAFT Advantage:**
- Pay-per-use ($29/doc) = cheaper for light users
- Free tier (12/month) = better than competitors
- Zero-knowledge = unique value prop
- No subscription = lower commitment
