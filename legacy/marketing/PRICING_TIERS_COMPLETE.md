## OVERVIEW: TWO-TIER STRUCTURE

TrueVow uses a **two-tier structure** (Free + Standard) for all products, with additional benefits for INTAKE users and Founding Members.

**Key Principle:** Founding Member is NOT a pricing tier — it's a discount/upgrade program applied after signup.

## 7. KEY PRICING PRINCIPLES

1. **Two-Tier Structure:** Free + Standard (recommended by 13/16 experts)
2. **Pay-Per-Performance:** Pay only when you win (INTAKE, SETTLE, DRAFT)
3. **No Monthly Minimums:** Zero commitment, cancel anytime
4. **Founding Member is NOT a Tier:** It's a discount program applied after signup
5. **INTAKE User Benefits:** Automatic discounts/free access to other products
6. **33% County Cap:** Protected competitive advantage (INTAKE only)
