### **1. ENHANCED ACCEPTANCE LANGUAGE (SECTION 1)**

**ToS #1 & #2 HAD:** Standard acceptance language

**ToS #3 ADDS/ENHANCES:**

✅ **1.1 "Irrevocably, Unconditionally, and Expressly Accept"**
- **Why:** More emphatic acceptance language
- **Language:** "By clicking 'I Accept,' creating an account, making payment, or otherwise accessing or using... you **irrevocably, unconditionally, and expressly accept and agree** to be bound by..."
- **Impact:** Stronger binding language, emphasizes irrevocability

✅ **1.4 Continued Use = Continued Acceptance - EXPLICIT**
- **Why:** ToS #1/#2 didn't have this explicit clause
- **Language:** "Your continued use of the Services following any amendment to these Terms constitutes your **ratification, reaffirmation, and acceptance** of such amendments"
- **Impact:** Prevents "I didn't accept the new terms" claims

✅ **1.5 Electronic Signature - E-SIGN ACT REFERENCE**
- **Why:** ToS #2 mentioned electronic signatures, but ToS #3 adds specific statute reference
- **Language:** "Your electronic acceptance (click-wrap) constitutes a **valid and binding signature** under the **U.S. Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (E-SIGN)** and equivalent state laws"
- **Impact:** Explicit legal foundation for electronic acceptance
