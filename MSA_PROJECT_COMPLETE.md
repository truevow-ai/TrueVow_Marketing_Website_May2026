## 🎓 **LESSONS LEARNED**

### **What Worked Well:**
1. **Iterative approach:** Analyzing one sample at a time allowed for detailed review
2. **Approval gates:** Getting user approval at each stage ensured alignment
3. **Comprehensive documentation:** Each improvement clearly numbered and explained
4. **Categorization:** Grouping improvements by theme aided understanding

### **Best Practices Applied:**
1. **Corporate structure clarity:** UAE/Nevis/Wyoming roles explicitly defined
2. **Dual arbitration:** Cost-effective small claims (AAA) + robust large claims (ICC)
3. **Operational precision:** Specific metrics (≥8 of 16, 30 days, SHA-256, etc.)
4. **Multiple acknowledgments:** Customer acknowledges risks in multiple sections
5. **Universal survival:** All critical obligations survive termination
