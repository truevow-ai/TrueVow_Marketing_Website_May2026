### **FUNCTIONS AND LIMITATIONS OF TRUEVOW SERVICES**

#### **1. Client Intake and Qualification (TrueVow INTAKE™)**

**What Happened:**
Your initial phone call was processed by TrueVow's automated intake system, which uses **deterministic, rule-based logic** (similar to a flow chart) to collect facts and determine if your matter falls within our practice areas and key qualification criteria.

**Important Limitations:**
- This system makes a **technical qualification only** (a "Qualified Booking")
- It **does not** provide legal analysis, legal advice, or guarantee that the Firm will ultimately take your case
- An attorney from **[Firm Name]** will independently review your matter and make the final decision on whether to represent you

**Confidentiality:**
The intake process is structured to preserve the attorney-client privilege regarding the information you disclose.

---

#### **2. Document Drafting (TrueVow DRAFT™)**

**What May Happen:**
If retained, the Firm may use the **TrueVow DRAFT™** module to generate **initial drafts** of legal documents (e.g., initial complaints, demand letters).

**Important Limitations:**
- These documents are **raw, preliminary drafts only**
- The Firm's licensed attorney is **required** to review, edit, verify the accuracy, and certify the final document before it is used in your representation or filed with any court
- **The attorney is solely responsible for the content and legal sufficiency of any final document**
- TrueVow is a software tool, not a legal advisor

---

#### **3. Settlement Data Visualization (TrueVow SETTLE™)**

**What May Happen:**
The Firm may use **TrueVow SETTLE™** to visualize data related to historical case results in similar matters.

**Important Limitations:**
- This tool provides **historical data** from public records and anonymized sources
- It **does not** predict the outcome of your specific case
- It **does not** guarantee a specific settlement amount or offer a valuation of your claim
- The Firm relies on the attorney's professional judgment, not the software, to advise you on settlement
- Many factors unique to your case (liability, comparative fault, venue, credibility) can significantly affect outcomes

**No Guarantee:**
Historical settlement ranges shown in this tool are **not** a guarantee, promise, or estimation of your recovery.
