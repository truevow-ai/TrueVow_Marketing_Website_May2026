What Benjamin Does During the Trial

Benjamin is not a generic receptionist and not a free-form legal chatbot. It is controlled intake automation built around your firm’s approved path.

Answers routed calls when enabled

Calls route based on your configured schedule, routing rules, and intake setup.

Qualifies the matter

Benjamin collects structured facts: incident type, timing, injury signals, treatment status, location, and urgency.

Grades the lead

Qualified leads are organized into grades (A+/A/B/C/D) so the firm can identify which opportunities deserve immediate attention.

Books or escalates

Benjamin can help route the next step: consultation booking, priority alert, follow-up queue, or structured review.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What happens after I apply?

    We review your firm fit, practice area, and county availability. If approved, we send you the onboarding preparation form before your onboarding call. Benjamin is activated only after setup, script review, calendar/routing configuration, and a successful test call.

    Is the trial free?

    The 90-day intake trial is approval-based. Trial pricing, pilot terms, and conversion details are confirmed during onboarding. No payment is collected on this page.

    Do I need to forward my calls?

    Yes. Benjamin handles routed calls based on your configured schedule, routing rules, and intake setup. You will need to configure call forwarding or provide phone routing access during onboarding.

    Is Benjamin a chatbot?

    No. Benjamin is not a free-form chatbot. Benjamin follows firm-approved intake paths, routing rules, escalation logic, and recording settings. It can support yes/no questions, structured responses, and approved open-ended prompts, but it is not designed to provide legal advice or make attorney judgment calls.

    What if my county is not available?

    County availability is reviewed during the application process. If your county is not yet supported, you may be placed on a waitlist and notified when availability opens.

    Is payment collected on this page?

    No. No payment is collected on this page. If approved, plan selection and billing happen inside the customer portal after onboarding.

    Can I buy directly from this page?

    No. TrueVow uses an approval-first process. Apply first, get reviewed, then activate and pay inside the customer portal if approved.
