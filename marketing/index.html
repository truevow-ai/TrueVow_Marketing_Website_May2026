Common Questions

Is Benjamin an answering service?

No. Answering services take messages and forward calls. Benjamin qualifies leads by practice area, flags lead priority, books consults, and triggers priority alerts for strong leads. It follows firm-approved intake paths, routing rules, and escalation logic instead of free-form chatbot behavior.

Is this a chatbot?

Benjamin uses deterministic intake logic for live-call decisions. It can support approved open-ended responses, but it is not designed to improvise legal advice or make attorney judgment calls.

Do I pay for every lead?

No. TrueVow INTAKE does not use public per-lead unlock fees. Every plan keeps intake records visible inside the portal. Benjamin may flag A+ opportunities based on firm-approved criteria, but the firm reviews and decides what to pursue.

Is Spanish included?

Yes. English and Spanish intake are both included at no extra cost. Callers are served in whichever language they prefer.

Can I use LEVERAGE or SETTLE without INTAKE?

Yes. LEVERAGE can be used standalone or at the ecosystem price with active INTAKE. SETTLE can be used per report by approved firms, while SETTLE Pro requires active INTAKE or LEVERAGE. When modules are connected, structured case data can flow through the TrueVow pipeline.
