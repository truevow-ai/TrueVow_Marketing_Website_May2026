A missed call does not appear in your books. A slow follow-up does not create an invoice. A weak intake process does not tell you which cases it lost.

The loss is invisible until your caseload feels lighter than it should.

FAQ

Is this ROI guaranteed?

No. This is a directional estimate only — not a promise, prediction, or guarantee. Many factors affect actual results, including call volume, practice area, jurisdiction, consultation quality, attorney follow-up, client selection, and firm operations.

Why use conservative assumptions?

Because inflated calculators destroy trust. Conservative defaults let you see a reasonable starting point, not a fantasy ceiling. You can adjust the inputs if your actual numbers are different.

Why include break-even cases?

Because break-even math is the cleanest way to evaluate intake infrastructure. The question is simple: how many signed cases does INTAKE need to help you capture for the base subscription to make economic sense?

Does this include A+ unlock overages?

No. The calculator uses base INTAKE subscription cost only. Each plan includes a set number of A+ unlocks. If you exceed those included unlocks, additional unlock fees may apply. See pricing for details.

Can INTAKE replace a receptionist or answering service?

Sometimes, but that is not the best way to evaluate it. INTAKE handles structured intake and booking. Some firms use it alongside existing staff; others reduce their answering service. The right question is whether your current intake system captures, qualifies, books, and routes the right opportunities before they go cold.
