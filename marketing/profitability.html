Complete Call Flow: From First Ring to Booking Confirmation

Every step is pre-defined, auditable, and Bar-compliant. No AI decisions. No creative answers. Just your approved rules, executed consistently. This is the actual script currently implemented for Personal Injury cases.

STEP 1: Call Answer & Initial Greeting

Benjamin: "Hi — I'm Benjamin, a virtual intake assistant for {law_firm_name}.

This call is private. No recording is made.

Nothing you say will be shared without your consent.

I'll ask you questions to help connect you with an attorney — I can't provide legal advice, only an attorney can.

We're here to understand what happened, so we can help. Let's get started...

Are you a new caller or returning?"

Compliance Note: Clear disclosure that Benjamin is a service provider to the law firm, not part of the firm. Proactive compliance with Rule 5.5, prevents expectation of legal advice.

STEP 2: Contact Information Collection (For New Callers)

Benjamin: "Thank you for reaching out. Let's gather some preliminary details to better understand your case. This will ensure the attorney is fully prepared to assist you during the consultation. Before we begin with the details, I'd like to capture your contact information — just in case we get disconnected or the call drops. This way, we can reach back out to you, book your consultation, and make sure you get the help you need. Your information is completely confidential and only used to contact you about your case. Is that okay with you? — Just say 'One' for Yes or 'Two' to Skip."

If Yes: "Thanks, may I have your first name. And if you don't mind Could you spell it out please?"

"{first_name}... Could you also spell out your last name as well?"

"Could i have our email address, and would you be kind to spell it out for accuracy please?"

"And finally would you be kind and provide me with your preferred contact number, rest assured your number will not be shared with any third party and will strictly be used for communication between the law firm and yourself?"

Compliance Note: Contact information is only collected with explicit permission. If caller skips, Benjamin proceeds to practice area selection.

STEP 3: Practice Area Selection

Benjamin: "To connect you with the right support — just let us know which type of case this is about. Just say the number from any of the following options:"

  • 'One' — if it's a car accident
  • 'Two' — if it's a slip and fall
  • 'Three' — if it's a workplace injury
  • 'Four' — if it's a dog bite
  • 'Five' — if it's due to a defective product
  • 'Six' — if it's another personal injury claim

"Just say the number — or repeat the case type."

Design Note: No open-ended questions. Only selections. Avoids asking "What happened?" — too traumatic. Gives control: they pick the label that fits.

STEP 4: Car Accident Qualification Questions (Q2-Q17)

If caller selects "One" (Car Accident), Benjamin proceeds through 16 sequential questions. All questions use yes/no or selection-based logic. No open-ended questions. No legal advice.

Complete Question Flow for Car Accident Cases:

  1. Q2: "I understand you need assistance with Car accident case. Now, tell me — was this in California?" (Yes/No/Not sure)
  2. Q3: "Sorry to hear that. When did the accident occur?" (Today/Within last week/More than a week ago/More than a month ago)
  3. Q4: "Was a police report filed at the scene? Just say the number" (Yes I filed it/No no officer came/Not sure I left the scene)
  4. Q5: "Did you or the other party receive a citation?" (Only asked if Q4 = Yes) (Yes I received/Yes other party received/No citations)
  5. Q6: "Were there any witnesses? Just say the number" (Yes I have names/Yes but don't have contact/No I was alone/Not sure)
  6. Q7: "Did your car sustain damage? Just say the number" (Minor/Moderate/Severe/Total loss/No damage but I was hurt/Not sure)
  7. Q8: "Have you seen a doctor for injuries from the accident? Just say the number" (Yes ER visit/Yes doctor's office/Yes physical therapy/Yes surgery/No not yet/Waiting for appointment)
  8. Q9: "What type of injuries did you sustain?" (Head Injury/Broken Bone/Cuts and Bruises/Whiplash/Multiple injury types/Other) — Skipped if Q8 = No not yet or Waiting
  9. Q10: "Are you still receiving medical treatment? Just say the number" (Yes/No) — Skipped if Q8 = No not yet or Waiting
  10. Q11: "Was the other party injured? Just say the number" (Yes Minor/Yes Severe/No Only I was hurt/Not sure)
  11. Q12: "Has the injury kept you from working? Just say the number" (Yes I'm on leave/Yes light duty only/No back to work/Self-employed income affected/Unemployed job prospects lost)
  12. Q13: "What kind of insurance coverage do you have? Just say the number" (Full Coverage/Liability Coverage/Uninsured or Underinsured Motorist/Commercial Policy/Don't know my policy)
  13. Q14: "Have you received an estimate for the car repairs?" (Yes/No)
  14. Q15: "Has the other party's insurer made an offer? Just say the number" (Yes here's the amount/Yes but I didn't accept/No silent/No I haven't contacted them)
  15. Q16: "If you're comfortable sharing, what range was the offer in?" (Under $25,000/$25,000–$50,000/Over $50,000)
  16. Q17: "Would you like to schedule a consultation? Just say the number" (Book my consult now/Call me back at a later time/Please send me the intake form first)

Compliance Safeguards: Never asks for names, SSNs, or medical details. Never asks for diagnosis — only treatment status. Never asks for VIN, photos, or repair cost. All answers are categorical. Questions are factual only — no legal advice.

STEP 5: Consultation Booking (Three Options)

OPTION A: "Book My Consult Now"

Benjamin: "What is your preferred date and time? Or I can suggest the next available slots. Would you prefer morning or afternoon? We have openings: Wednesday at 9:30 AM or Wednesday at 2:00 PM. Which one works better?"

After response: "Thank you. Your consultation is confirmed for [Day] at [Time]. We'll send a secure message to your phone with: A link to complete your intake form, Our Engagement Letter — explaining how we work, What to bring to your first meeting. No voicemail. No pressure. Just clarity. If anything changes, you can reschedule through the link. Have a good day. And rest easy — we're here when you're ready."

OPTION B: "Call Me Back Later"

Benjamin: "We understand. There's no obligation. We won't leave a voicemail. We won't send an email. We won't save your number beyond this session. If you choose to reach out again — whether today or in six months — we'll be here. Until you choose for us to call you at a preferred time of yours?"

After response: "Have a good day. And rest easy — we're here when you're ready."

⚠️ Do NOT store phone beyond session. Only call once — unless reconfirmed.

OPTION C: "Send Me Intake Form & Engagement Letter"

Benjamin: "Thank you for sharing. We've captured your request. I'm sending two secure links to your phone via SMS: A private link to complete your intake — only you can access it, An Engagement Letter explaining how we work — no jargon, just clarity. If you choose to move forward, you should submit the intake form and our attorney will reach out within 24 hours or you can choose to book a consultation yourself. Until then — rest easy. Your privacy is protected. Your story was never recorded. And nothing you said will be shared unless you confirm. We're here when you're ready."

STEP 6: SMS Confirmation

For Booked Consultations: SMS sent with appointment details, secure intake form link, Engagement Letter link, what to bring, and expiration notice (7 days).

For Intake Form Requests: SMS sent with private intake form link and Engagement Letter link. No tracking. No login. No risk.

System Action: Complete intake log sent to firm with all questions asked and answers given. Timestamped. Auditable.

STEP 7: Call Completion

Benjamin: "You're all set! We'll see you [Day] at [Time]. If you need to reschedule, just call this number. Have a great day!"

Result: Qualified booking created (if applicable). Complete intake log available for attorney review. No legal advice given. No promises made. No audio stored. Just factual information collection and appointment scheduling.

Key Compliance Safeguards:

  • Every question is pre-approved by you during onboarding
  • No legal advice is ever given — only factual questions
  • Complete audit trail of every call is available
  • All decisions follow your rules — no AI creativity
  • Caller is clearly informed that Benjamin is not an attorney
  • You only pay for qualified bookings (≥8 questions answered, within statute, practice area match)
