Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing & Billing
How does INTAKE pricing work?
Routed calls are handled when enabled, and qualified lead records are organized by grade. You see all leads (A, B, C, D). You only pay to unlock A+ leads — high-priority opportunities based on injury, treatment, jurisdiction, timing, and liability signals.

Solo ($199/mo): 3 A+ unlocks included, 500 routed voice minutes, $49/additional A+ unlock
Growth ($599/mo): 11 A+ unlocks included, 1,500 routed voice minutes, $39/additional A+ unlock
Team ($1,499/mo): 33 A+ unlocks included, 4,500 routed voice minutes, $29/additional A+ unlock

A, B, C, and D leads are visible without unlock. Only A+ leads require an unlock. Included unlocks reset monthly and do not roll over.
What's the difference between A, B, C, and D leads?
A+ leads: High-priority opportunities based on injury, treatment, jurisdiction, timing, and liability signals — these require an unlock.

B/C leads: Lower-value or borderline cases — always visible, always free.

D leads: Spam, wrong practice area, expired statute — filtered out automatically.

You see every lead. You only pay to unlock the high-priority opportunities.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Plan changes can be made through the customer portal after approval. Start with Solo, and request a change when your volume or workflow changes. Availability may depend on onboarding capacity.
Is Spanish really included free?
Yes. Benjamin supports fluent Spanish intake at no additional cost across all INTAKE plans. Spanish is included, not sold separately.
How It Works
Is Benjamin a chatbot?
No. Benjamin is not a free-form chatbot. Benjamin follows firm-approved intake paths, routing rules, escalation logic, and recording settings. It can support yes/no questions, structured responses, and approved open-ended prompts, but it is not designed to provide legal advice or make attorney judgment calls.
How does LEVERAGE work?
LEVERAGE structures your case after intake, before settlement.

Most firms don't lose cases. They lose value inside the case. LEVERAGE helps you:
• Identify key damages you might miss
• Structure your position for clearer negotiation
• Prepare for negotiation with data

If INTAKE helps win the case, LEVERAGE helps structure the value story.
How does SETTLE work?
Enter your case details — injury type, treatment, jurisdiction, surgery, lost wages — and get a settlement range with confidence level and comparable signals.

Used only when decisions matter — before demand or settlement.

See comparable settlement signals before you negotiate. Confidence is clearly labeled.

Compliance & Security
What about compliance and data handling?
TrueVow is designed for controlled data handling, not zero-knowledge storage. Depending on firm configuration, the system may process contact details, structured intake answers, lead grades, booking details, risk flags, optional recordings, and optional transcripts.

Recording and transcript generation are optional and controlled by firm policy. Case analysis workflows do not require client identifiers where they are not needed. TrueVow is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice.
Add-Ons & Unlocks
Are all leads locked?
No. A, B, C, and D leads are visible without unlock. Only A+ leads require an unlock.
Do included A+ unlocks roll over?
No. Included A+ unlocks reset monthly and do not roll over.
Is FOLLOW-UP available on Solo?
No. FOLLOW-UP is available on Growth and Team plans. Solo is designed for simple one-attorney intake coverage.
Is COMPLETE the same as FOLLOW-UP?
No. FOLLOW-UP handles outbound callbacks, reminders, and follow-up attempts. COMPLETE is a secure post-call packet for collecting additional details, uploads, and consult-prep information after the live call.
Are recordings and transcripts included?
No. Recordings and transcripts are optional add-ons for Growth and Team plans. They are configured during onboarding based on firm policy, disclosure requirements, and jurisdiction.
Who Is This For?
What practice areas does TrueVow support?
TrueVow is currently positioned for solo and small plaintiff-side PI firms. Additional practice areas may be reviewed during onboarding, but the public product is optimized around PI intake, qualification, case value structure, and settlement decision support.
Why does TrueVow publish pricing when many legal AI platforms require a demo?
TrueVow is built for solo and small PI firms, not enterprise procurement committees. We publish starting prices so firm owners can understand the basic economics before applying. Final activation still happens after approval inside the customer portal because setup, county availability, recording policy, calendar configuration, and product fit must be reviewed first.
Do you serve large firms or enterprise?
No. TrueVow is built exclusively for solo and small law firms (≤21 attorneys). We don't serve BigLaw or enterprise legal departments.
