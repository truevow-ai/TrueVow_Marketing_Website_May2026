Common Concerns (Addressed)

"Is CONNECT really bar-compliant?"

Yes. CONNECT is designed for bar compliance from day one. We comply with all ABA Model Rules (1.5(e), 5.4, 7.2(b), 1.6, 1.7). CONNECT requires pre-clearance from state bars before launch. Is CONNECT bar-compliant?
Yes. CONNECT is designed for bar compliance from day one. We comply with all ABA Model Rules (1.5(e), 5.4, 7.2(b), 1.6, 1.7). CONNECT requires pre-clearance from state bars (e.g., FL Bar Ethics Opinion 24-1) before launch. Do not use until officially launched and state-approved. Join the waitlist to be notified when CONNECT is available in your state.
How does CONNECT handle fee division?
CONNECT does NOT facilitate fee division. We are an introductory platform only. You and the receiving attorney enter into a separate, written referral agreement that complies with state bar rules (ABA Model Rule 1.5(e)). CONNECT does NOT participate in or facilitate fee splits. Attorneys are solely responsible for ensuring compliance with fee division rules.
What information does CONNECT share?
CONNECT shares only non-privileged information: practice area, jurisdiction, case type. We do NOT share client names, case facts, privileged communications, or legal strategy. CONNECT uses zero-knowledge architecture - we minimize data sharing to protect attorney-client privilege.
Can I connect with medical providers?
Yes, but with strict compliance safeguards. CONNECT can facilitate attorney-to-medical provider connections, but there can be NO fee arrangements between attorneys and medical providers (Rule 5.4). If you share PHI with medical providers via CONNECT, a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is required. CONNECT provides BAA templates, but attorneys are solely responsible for executing BAAs and obtaining client consent.
When will CONNECT be available?
CONNECT is currently in pre-launch. We require pre-clearance from state bars before launch (e.g., FL Bar Ethics Opinion 24-1). Public launch is scheduled for Q1 2027, pending state bar approvals. Join the waitlist to get early access and be notified when CONNECT is available in your state.
