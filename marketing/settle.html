Frequently Asked Questions
What if SETTLE does not have data for my county or injury type?

When county-specific data is limited, SETTLE uses the closest available comparable signals, including state, region, injury type, treatment pattern, medical bill range, and liability profile. The report clearly labels confidence.

If SETTLE can generate a useful directional report, you may choose to run it. If there is not enough data to produce a meaningful report, you are not charged.

Do I pay if the confidence is low?

High and medium confidence reports are charged normally. For low-confidence directional reports, SETTLE shows you the confidence level and limitation note first — you choose whether to proceed. If there isn't enough data to produce a useful report, you pay nothing.

Is SETTLE independent? Or is it controlled by insurers?

SETTLE is designed as attorney-controlled decision support. It is not an insurer-controlled product. Settlement intelligence is built from public sources and permissioned attorney contributions where available. SETTLE provides structured range context and confidence labels; attorneys remain responsible for settlement judgment and client advice.

Can I cancel anytime?

Per Case reports are pay-per-use with no commitment. SETTLE Pro has a 3-month minimum and can be canceled anytime after through the customer portal. If SETTLE cannot produce a meaningful report, you are not charged for that report.

How does SETTLE Pro rollover work?

Unused included reports roll into your Pro rollover bank while your subscription remains active, capped at 63 reports. Your rollover bank resets to zero at each annual subscription anniversary and does not carry over after cancellation.

Usage order: (1) current monthly included reports, (2) rollover bank, (3) paid overage at $25/report.

What does a SETTLE report include?

Every SETTLE report includes: settlement range, confidence level, comparable case signals (injury type, treatment pattern, medical bill range, liability profile), and data limitation disclosure. County-specific data when available; closest comparable signals otherwise.

Is SETTLE legal advice?

No. SETTLE outputs are decision-support tools, not settlement guarantees or legal advice. You stay in control of every negotiation and settlement decision. SETTLE gives you data. You make the call.

How does SETTLE Pro work?

SETTLE Pro is $299/month with 15 reports included. Available to firms with active INTAKE or LEVERAGE subscriptions. Additional reports beyond 15 are $25 each. Unused included reports roll into your rollover bank (capped at 63) while your subscription remains active. 3-month minimum, cancel anytime after.
