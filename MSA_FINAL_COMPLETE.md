## **SCHEDULE B: QUALIFIED BOOKING CRITERIA & FOUNDING MEMBER PROGRAM**

### **B.1 Qualified Booking Criteria**

A Booking is "Qualified" only if ALL of the following are met:

1. **Qualification Questions:** Caller answers **≥ 8 of 16** pre-defined qualification questions
2. **Consent:** Caller provides valid consent to book consultation
3. **Contact Information:** Valid name and phone number provided
4. **SMS Confirmation:** Appointment confirmed via SMS
5. **Statute of Limitations:** Incident within statute of limitations for Customer's jurisdiction
6. **No Duplicate:** Not a duplicate of existing lead within **30 days**
7. **Practice Area Match:** Matter within Customer's designated practice areas

### **B.2 What Customer Does NOT Pay For**

- Spam calls or robocalls
- Wrong numbers
- Existing clients
- Practice areas Customer does not handle
- Callers who hang up before completing intake
- Duplicate bookings (same caller within 30 days)

### **B.3 Founding Member Pricing Lock**

**Rate:** $25 per Qualified Booking (vs. standard $29)  
**Lock Period:** Until July 4, 2026 (minimum), may be extended at Provider's discretion  
**Eligibility:** See Founding Member Definition (Section 1.5)

### **B.4 Founding Member Activity Gates**

To retain 3 free monthly bookings and $25 rate, Customer must satisfy **ONE** of the following each month:

**(i)** Generate **≥ 1 paid booking**, OR  
**(ii)** Submit **≥ 1 settlement data point** to TrueVow SETTLE (once launched), OR  
**(iii)** Generate **≥ 3 qualified attorney referrals** per year

**Failure:** 3 consecutive months → **permanent loss** of free bookings + $25 rate reverts to standard $29 after 12-month grace period. **Status cannot be reinstated.**

### **B.5 Free Bookings (Standard)**

- **Quantity:** 11 free Qualified Bookings for new customers
- **Expiry:** Must be used within 12 months
- **Restrictions:** Non-transferable, non-refundable, non-cashable
