- ✅ Limitation of liability in Privacy Policy

- ✅ Zero-Knowledge Architecture (detailed specifications)
- ✅ Ephemeral processing (TTL ≤900s, in-RAM, purged before call end)

- ✅ Customer segmentation (isolated VPC, separate schemas, unique KMS keys)

- ✅ Cryptographic shredding with checksum verification
- ✅ Backup recovery re-flagging
- ✅ Immutable logging (CloudTrail + Loki, 365 days, no content)
