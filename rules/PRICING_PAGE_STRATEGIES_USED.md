## 🎯 KEY STRATEGIES THAT DROVE CONVERSION

### 1. **Trust-First Approach** (Jill Konrath, Chris Voss)
- "I've been burned before" objection addressed FIRST
- Removes biggest psychological barrier

### 2. **Value-Based Foundation** (Alex Hormozi, Rafi Muhammad)
- "Capturing $5,150 you would have lost"
- Loss aversion framing

### 3. **Movement Positioning** (Seth Godin)
- "Community members, not customers"
- "Joining a movement to level the playing field"

### 4. **Authentic Trust-Building** (David Ogilvy)
- No scarcity tactics
- No manipulation
- Honest, respectful copy

### 5. **Optimal Objection Order** (Conversion Psychology)
- Trust → Ability → Money
- Removes barriers in logical sequence

### 6. **Price Anchoring** (Rafi Muhammad)
- Comparison section ABOVE pricing
- Shows expensive alternatives first

### 7. **Risk Reversal** (Alex Hormozi)
- 11 FREE bookings
- Cancel anytime
- No monthly minimums
