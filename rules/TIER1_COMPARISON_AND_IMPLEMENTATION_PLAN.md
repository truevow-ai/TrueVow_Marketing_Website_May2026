# Tier 1 Recommendations Comparison & Implementation Plan

## Analysis 1 (Psychology & Decision Science) - Tier 1

1. **Loss Aversion Framing** (Rafi Muhammad, Daniel Cho)
   - "Stop losing $47,652/year" not "Save $47,652/year"
   - Frame all ROI as losses avoided
   - Expected impact: +12-18% conversion

2. **Emotional ROI Anchors** (Martin Lindstrom)
   - "Reclaim 15 hours/week with family" (emotional)
   - "Wake up to booked consultations" (sensory, visual)
   - Expected impact: +10-15% conversion

3. **Preemptive Skepticism Labeling** (Chris Voss, Brian Tracy)
   - "You're probably skeptical. That's smart. Here's the verified math."
   - Addresses "too good to be true" objection
   - Expected impact: +8-12% conversion

4. **Interactive ROI Calculator** (Danilo Zatta, Eugene Schwartz)
   - Let them input their numbers
   - Creates ownership of the calculation
   - Expected impact: +15-20% conversion

5. **Time-Based ROI Visualization** (Jill Konrath, Daniel Cho)
   - Month 1, Month 3, Month 6, Year 1
   - Shows compounding value over time
   - Expected impact: +10-14% conversion
