## ✅ **7 IMPROVEMENTS INTEGRATED**

### **#1 - Intentional Repetition Doctrine**
✓ Added preamble to 5 sections (Services, Customer Obligations, Data Security, Warranties, Liability, Indemnification, Termination)  
✓ Prevents ambiguity arguments in court  
✓ Explicit statement that repetition is strategic, not error

### **#2 - Customer Acknowledgment (Core Logic)**
✓ Added to Sections 2 (Services), 9 (Warranties), 11 (Indemnification)  
✓ Explicit: "Core Logic operates via deterministic FSM"  
✓ Explicit: "Does not employ generative AI, LLMs, or probabilistic inference"  
✓ Creates estoppel defense against "AI failure" claims

### **#3 - Customer Acknowledgment (Ancillary ML Tools)**
✓ Added to Sections 2 (Services), 9 (Warranties), 11 (Indemnification)  
✓ Explicit: "Used solely for non-legal, operational purposes"  
✓ Explicit: "Do not influence legal qualification, document content, or case strategy"  
✓ Shields Provider from ML tool vendor errors

### **#4 - Full Responsibility Transfer**
✓ Added to Sections 5 (Customer Obligations), 9 (Warranties)  
✓ "Customer assumes full responsibility for all reliance, decisions, or legal actions"  
✓ Eliminates shared responsibility arguments

### **#5 - Limited Warranty Declaration**
✓ Added to Section 9 (Warranties and Disclaimers)  
✓ "Provider makes no warranties beyond those expressly stated herein"  
✓ Bright-line rule for warranty scope

### **#6 - Preemptive Indemnification Acknowledgment**
✓ Added to Sections 5 (Customer Obligations), 11 (Indemnification)  
✓ Customer acknowledges indemnity obligation upfront in multiple places  
✓ Harder to challenge with multiple acknowledgments  
✓ Covers malpractice, compliance failures, ethical breaches

### **#7 - Universal Survival Clause**
✓ Added to 7 sections:
  - Section 5 (Customer Obligations)
  - Section 6 (Data Security)
  - Section 7 (IP)
  - Section 8 (Confidentiality)
  - Section 9 (Warranties)
  - Section 10 (Liability)
  - Section 11 (Indemnification)
  - Section 12 (Termination)
  - Section 13 (Dispute Resolution)
✓ Eliminates post-termination ambiguity
