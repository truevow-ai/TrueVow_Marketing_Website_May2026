## **3. PLAIN-ENGLISH SUMMARY OF OUR DATA MODEL**

### **3.1 Core Logic (Deterministic; No LLM)**

- **No generative AI** and **no probabilistic inference** drives intake qualifications, practice-area routing, or booking rules
- Deterministic, **finite-state-machine (FSM)** flows determine what is asked/recorded and when, using Firm-configured scripts and thresholds
- **Outputs are administrative** (create booking; mark qualified/non-qualified per Firm rules) and **not legal advice**

### **3.2 Ancillary ML Tools = Operational Only (Not Legal)**

We may use reputable third-party technologies for **operational purposes only**, including:

**Speech & Audio:**
- Speech-to-text (STT): Fast-Whisper, DeepGram, Silero ASR
- Text-to-speech (TTS): ElevenLabs, Cartesia
- Voice Activity Detection (VAD): Silero VAD, WebRTC VAD

**Infrastructure & Development:**
- Authentication: Auth0, Okta (OAuth 2.0, SAML, MFA)
- Database: Supabase, PlanetScale, Redis (query, cache, store)
- Workflow: n8n, LangGraph (data routing, orchestration)
- CDN/Edge: Cloudflare, AWS CloudFront (content delivery, DDoS)
- Monitoring: Sentry, Datadog, PagerDuty (logs, metrics, alerts)
- DevOps: GitHub, Cursor, Vercel v0 (code, deploy, UI generation)

**These tools are:**
- ✅ **Modular and replaceable**
- ✅ Used to improve **performance, reliability, accessibility, or developer efficiency**
- ✅ **Never** used to automate or substitute **legal judgment**
- ✅ Subject to **contractual guardrails** (DPAs) prohibiting training on Firm Client Data without explicit opt-in

### **3.3 Categories of Data We Handle**

**1. Operational/Account Data (Controller):**
- Firm name, admin contact info, bar numbers, billing address
- Payment tokens (never full credit card numbers)
- Usage telemetry linked to admin interactions
- Partner/affiliate records
- Firm profile, office addresses, VAT/EIN

**2. Firm Client Data (Processor):**
- INTAKE answers (as defined by Firm's custom scripts)
- Caller name and phone number
- Booking confirmations
- **Only if Retention Toggle enabled:** Call audio and/or transcripts for up to 7 days

**3. Telemetry & Security Logs (Controller/Processor):**
- Pseudonymous event logs (timestamps, status codes, API latency, auth outcomes)
- CDN/edge metrics
- Short-lived cache keys/hashes for performance
- Error traces for troubleshooting (minimized)

**4. SETTLE Inputs/Outputs (Controller/Processor):**
- Aggregated, **de-identified** historical docket analytics
- **No PHI or client identifiers** stored
- SETTLE **does not** predict outcomes

### **3.4 Sources of Data**

**1. You / Firm Admins:**  
Account setup, configuration files, intake scripts, booking rules, document variables

**2. Callers:**  
Information callers volunteer during Firm's scripted intake flows

**3. Systems:**  
Automatically collected operational telemetry, security events, short-lived performance caches

**4. Public Sources:**  
Public dockets and court records used for **de-identified** SETTLE visualizations

### **3.5 Why We Process Personal Data (Legal Bases)**

**Contract Performance** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))  
To deliver deterministic intake, create bookings, generate draft documents for Firm review, render SETTLE visualizations, provide support, maintain availability

**Legitimate Interests** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))  
Security, fraud prevention, observability, service improvement, usage analytics on admin interactions, vendor management—balanced against user rights with opt-outs where required

**Consent** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))  
- Optional **Retention Toggle** (temporary storage of call audio/transcripts for up to 7 days)
- Certain cookies/marketing (where required)
- **Any ML training by a third party** (prohibited unless explicit opt-in and law permits)

**Legal Obligations** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c))  
Tax and accounting records, breach notifications, court orders, sanctions/export-control compliance

**Special Category Health Data** (GDPR Art. 9(2)(h))  
For any incidental health-related data captured during injury-law intake, processing is necessary for healthcare purposes carried out by a health professional (the Firm's attorney) subject to professional secrecy

### **3.6 What We Do NOT Do**

**We do NOT:**
- ❌ Use LLMs or generative models to make or influence legal intake decisions
- ❌ Sell personal data (as "sale" is defined under state privacy laws)
- ❌ Engage in cross-context behavioral advertising without required opt-outs
- ❌ Retain call audio/transcripts by default (storage occurs **only** if Retention Toggle enabled)
- ❌ Allow subprocessors to train on Firm Client Data (absent explicit, documented opt-in)
- ❌ Send marketing to your callers/clients
- ❌ Share data with insurers, marketers, data brokers, or opposing counsel
