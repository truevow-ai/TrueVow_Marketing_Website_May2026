5. Product Scope

5.1 INTAKE

INTAKE is designed to help law firms capture, structure, route, and review potential intake opportunities.

Depending on plan and configuration, INTAKE may support:

  • routed call handling;
  • Benjamin intake flows;
  • selected intake templates;
  • structured intake questions;
  • intake or opportunity records;
  • priority flags or intake grades;
  • booking support;
  • routing and alert settings;
  • optional recording or transcript settings;
  • portal review;
  • integrations or exports where supported.

Benjamin follows selected intake templates, onboarding settings, routing settings, escalation boundaries, and firm configuration. Benjamin is not designed to provide legal advice or replace attorney judgment.

Default onboarding uses template-based intake flows. Fully custom intake workflows, custom prompts, custom branches, or custom practice-specific logic require separate scoping, pricing, validation, and testing.

5.2 LEVERAGE

LEVERAGE is designed to help structure case information, damages, deadlines, demand readiness, and related case-preparation materials for attorney review.

LEVERAGE is not case management software, does not replace legal strategy, and does not provide legal advice.

The Firm remains responsible for reviewing all information, deadlines, calculations, summaries, and case materials before relying on them.

5.3 SETTLE

SETTLE is designed to provide confidence-labeled settlement range context based on available comparable signals, public information, contributed data where applicable, or other supported data sources.

SETTLE does not guarantee settlement value, predict outcomes, provide legal advice, recommend settlement strategy, or replace attorney judgment.

If available data is insufficient, SETTLE may decline to generate a report or may provide only limited directional context with confidence labels and data limitation disclosures.

5.4 VERIFY

VERIFY is an optional platform audit feature that may generate tamper-evident records for covered platform actions, such as timestamps, selected field values, event metadata, or record hashes. VERIFY records may help support internal review, audit documentation, and integrity checks. VERIFY does not provide legal advice, does not prove legal or bar compliance, does not guarantee regulatory compliance, and does not replace attorney supervision, firm review, or professional judgment.
