4. The Services

TrueVow uses controlled, template-based intake workflows with deterministic routing, validation rules, and limited third-party processing for speech, messaging, infrastructure, and operational support. The Services follow firm-approved intake paths, routing rules, escalation logic, and configuration. They are not designed to provide legal advice or make attorney judgment calls.

4.1 INTAKE

When enabled and available, INTAKE can answer routed calls, collect intake information, create structured records, grade leads, support consultation booking, and send priority alerts — all according to the selected intake template and your firm’s configuration. INTAKE is activated only after onboarding, script review, calendar/routing configuration, and a successful test call.

4.2 LEVERAGE

LEVERAGE supports case workflow tasks such as damages calculation, deadline tracking, and structured case records. It can be used standalone or, where you maintain an active INTAKE subscription, at the ecosystem price with structured data flowing between products.

4.3 SETTLE

SETTLE displays historical and aggregated settlement information for descriptive reference. SETTLE provides descriptive statistics only; it does not predict outcomes, provide legal advice, or evaluate liability. You are responsible for how you use SETTLE information and for not submitting privileged or confidential client information except as expressly permitted.

4.4 VERIFY (Compliance Certificates)

VERIFY is an optional compliance capability that can generate blockchain-anchored (OpenTimestamps) cryptographic timestamps and certificates for covered actions, providing a tamper-evident, immutable audit record. VERIFY certificates evidence the integrity and timing of the specific fields that were recorded; they do not provide legal advice or guarantee compliance, and you remain responsible for your own professional and regulatory obligations.
